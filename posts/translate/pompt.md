# I turned Stephen Covey's 7 Habits into AI prompts and it changed everything

> 我把史蒂芬·柯维的《高效能人士的七个习惯》变成了 AI 提示词，这改变了一切

---

I've been obsessed with Stephen Covey's 7 Habits lately and realized these principles make incredible AI prompts.

> 最近我迷上了史蒂芬·柯维的《高效能人士的七个习惯》，我发现这些原则可以变成非常强大的 AI 提示词。

It's like having a personal effectiveness coach in your pocket:

> 就像你口袋里装了一个高效能教练一样：

---

### 1. Ask "What's within my control here?"

> 1. 问自己：“这里有哪些是我能控制的？”

Perfect for overwhelm or frustration. AI helps you separate what you can influence from what you can't.

> 这个提示非常适合在你感到不知所措或沮丧时使用。AI 会帮你区分哪些是你可以影响的，哪些不是。

_"I'm stressed about the economy. What's within my control here?"_

> “我对经济感到焦虑。我能控制的部分是什么？”

Instantly shifts focus to actionable steps.

> 这个问题能立刻把你的注意力转向可执行的行动。

---

### 2. Use "Help me begin with the end in mind"

> 2. 使用：“帮我以终为始地思考”

Game-changer for any decision.

> 对任何决策来说，这都是一个颠覆性的思维方式。

_"I'm choosing a career path. Help me begin with the end in mind."_

> “我在选择职业方向。帮我以终为始地思考。”

AI walks you through visualizing your ideal future and working backwards to today.

> AI 会引导你想象理想的未来，并倒推回今天应该采取的行动。

---

### 3. Say "What should I put first?"

> 3. 说：“我应该先做什么？”

The ultimate prioritization prompt.

> 这是终极的优先级提示。

When everything feels urgent, this cuts through the noise.

> 当一切看起来都很紧急时，它能帮你理清头绪。

_"I have 10 projects due. What should I put first?"_

> “我有 10 个项目要交，哪一个该先做？”

AI becomes your priority coach.

> AI 会变成你的优先级教练。

---

### 4. Add "How can we both win here?"

> 4. 加上：“我们怎样才能双赢？”

Perfect for conflicts or negotiations.

> 这个提示非常适合用于冲突或谈判场景。

Instead of win-lose thinking, AI finds creative solutions where everyone benefits.

> 它能帮助你跳出输赢思维，找到让双方都受益的创造性方案。

_"My roommate wants quiet, I want music. How can we both win here?"_

> “我的室友想要安静，我想听音乐。我们怎样才能双赢？”

---

### 5. Ask "What am I missing by not really listening?"

> 5. 问：“我因为没有真正倾听而错过了什么？”

This one's sneaky powerful.

> 这个问题的威力出乎意料。

Paste in an email or describe a conversation, then ask this.

> 你可以把一封邮件或一段对话贴给 AI，然后问这个问题。

AI spots the underlying needs and emotions you might have missed completely.

> AI 会识别出你可能完全忽略的潜在需求和情绪。

---

### 6. Use "How can I combine these strengths?"

> 6. 使用：“我该如何结合这些优势？”

When you're stuck on a problem, list your resources/skills and ask this.

> 当你在问题上卡住时，列出你的资源或技能，然后问这个问题。

AI finds creative combinations you wouldn't see.

> AI 会找到一些你自己想不到的创造性组合。

_"I'm good at writing and coding. How can I combine these strengths?"_

> “我擅长写作和编程。我该如何结合这些优势？”

---

### 7. Say "Help me sharpen the saw on this"

> 7. 说：“帮我磨磨锯子”

The self-renewal prompt.

> 这是一个关于自我更新的提示。

AI designs improvement plans for any skill or area.

> AI 会为任何技能或领域设计改进计划。

_"Help me sharpen the saw on my communication skills."_

> “帮我磨练一下我的沟通技巧。”

Gets you specific, sustainable growth strategies.

> 它会给出具体且可持续的成长策略。

---

The magic happens because these habits are designed to shift your perspective.

> 这些提示之所以神奇，是因为“七个习惯”本身就是为了改变你的思维视角而设计的。

AI amplifies this by processing your situation through these mental models instantly.

> 而 AI 能即时地把你的情况通过这些思维模型来分析，从而放大这种效果。

---

### Try This: Chain them together

> 试试这个：把它们连起来用

_"What's within my control for this career change? Help me begin with the end in mind. What should I put first?"_

> “面对这次职业转变，我能控制的是什么？帮我以终为始地思考。我该先做什么？”

It's like having a full effectiveness coaching session.

> 就像你和一位高效能教练进行了一整场辅导一样。

---

Most people use AI for quick answers.

> 大多数人只是用 AI 来获得快速答案。

These prompts make it think about your problems the way highly effective people do.

> 而这些提示能让 AI 以高效能人士的思维方式来思考你的问题。

---

What's your biggest challenge right now?

> 你现在最大的挑战是什么？

Try running it through one of these and see what happens.

> 试着用其中一个提示来问问 AI，看看会发生什么。
