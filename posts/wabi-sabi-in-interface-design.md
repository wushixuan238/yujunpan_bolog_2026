Wabi-sabi teaches us to appreciate the imperfect, impermanent, and incomplete. How might this ancient aesthetic philosophy inform modern interface design?

Consider:
- Embracing whitespace as breathing room
- Allowing asymmetry to create visual interest
- Designing for graceful degradation

Perfection is not the goal. Authenticity is.
